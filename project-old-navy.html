Old Navy — Free Concept

• Biannual Cadence
Spatial Design
Concept Development
Retail

Developed a speculative spatial concept for Old Navy — reimagining the in-store shopping experience through modular environments, expressive signage systems, and a refreshed visual language suited to the brand's inclusive, playful identity. The concept explored how flexibility and personality could coexist in a mass-market retail setting, offering a scalable framework that could grow with the brand.
