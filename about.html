About

Where craft meets production.

Designing with purpose, producing with precision.

I lead end-to-end web production — turning strategy and design into polished, high-performing websites. My work sits at the intersection of visual precision and technical execution.

I thrive in collaborative environments where teams move fast but care about the details. Whether managing timelines, overseeing QA, or rolling up my sleeves in the CMS, I make sure every launch lands well.

Good web production is invisible — things just work, load fast, and look exactly right. That's the standard I hold myself to.

Background

The full
story.

I studied Graphic Design at York University and Sheridan College, graduating with a Bachelor of Design with Honours. Drawn west by a love of fashion and visual culture, I continued at the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising in San Francisco, completing a Visual Communications certificate and graduating Summa Cum Laude.

My career began at Levi Strauss, where I produced global merchandising assets and developed a deep appreciation for brand consistency at scale. I then joined Conduit Creative Studio in Los Angeles as a Concept Designer, developing multi-channel marketing campaigns and learning how ideas move from brief to reality.

For six years, I co-founded and led creative direction at Lot, Stock and Barrel — a collective of creatives based in LA specializing in apparel, spatial, and graphic design. We partnered with brands to design and produce end-to-end experiential moments: retail activations, product launches, and music festivals. I managed everything from concept through execution, often producing customization experiences on-site.

In 2021, I joined Block (Square Inc.) as Digital and Web Production Lead — overseeing a 14-person team and managing publishing operations across 17,000+ pages in 8 countries and 11 locales. I reduced delivery cycle times from 5–6 months down to weeks, led company-wide launches involving 40+ teams, and drove measurable results including a +$500M annualized incremental NVA contribution.

Now back in Toronto, I'm eager to bring this breadth — strategic, creative, and operational — to companies both big and small. My goal is always to find creative solutions that respect the values and integrity of each brand.
